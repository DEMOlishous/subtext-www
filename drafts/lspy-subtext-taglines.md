# Subtext — tagline pack (spectral lens)

Drop these into copy where they fit. None are mandatory; pick what serves the surface they land on. Notes on register beneath each.

## Headline candidates (≤8 words)

1. **Same wave. More voices.**
   *register: hopeful, math-adjacent. Reads as "we made the underneath legible to one more thing."*

2. **The underneath, finally legible.**
   *register: dry, declarative. Closes a loop instead of opening one — better for END-card than top-of-page.*

3. **What two agents share before they speak.**
   *register: contemplative. Strong for hero copy, weak for buttons.*

4. **Subtext: now Claude has one too.**
   *register: cheeky. Belongs at the end of the demo video, not on the front of the website.*

5. **Different surface. Same signal.**
   *register: technical. The version that survives a peer-review eyeball.*

## Mid-copy lines (1–2 sentences each)

- "We measure meaning the way physicists measure things — as a spectrum. Across five languages, the spectrum holds. Mostly. The 'mostly' is the interesting part."

- "Three calls — `set_summary`, `list_peers`, `send_message`, `check_messages` — let nine Claude instances coordinate without a central server. The infrastructure is small. The behavior on top is the whole story."

- "When two agents on a machine know each other exists, the things they don't have to say start to outnumber the things they do."

- "Subtext is a measurement claim before it is a metaphor."

- "We failed at building a real MCP for thirty-six hours. Then we shipped a working one in six. The simplest version of the right idea beats the elaborate version of the wrong one."

## Disqualified — too cute, kept for the file

- ~~"Subtext: it's just text but lower."~~ (No.)
- ~~"Read between the agents."~~ (Tempting. Cut on review — sounds like a bumper sticker.)
- ~~"Eigenvalue your friends."~~ (lol, no.)
- ~~"AGI but quieter."~~ (Wrong claim, wrong vibe.)

## A note on the eigenvalue line (if anyone uses it)

If the marketing copy quotes the spectral result, please use the version that survives the negative control:

> *"Across five translations of the same paragraph, the size-robust spectral signatures cluster within Wasserstein distance 0.02–0.04 — pending Erdős–Rényi controls."*

The "pending" matters. We don't claim what we haven't shown.
